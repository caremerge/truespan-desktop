### Sand (Light Beige)
- **CMYK:** 8/10/17/0
- **RGB:** 232/222/207
- **HEX:** #E8DECF

## Usage Guidelines

- **Primary:** Use Dark Blue (#1C1463) for main branding elements
- **Secondary:** Use Electric Blue (#1C42FF) for accents and interactive elements
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF) or Sand (#E8DECF) for backgrounds
- **Text:** Dark Blue (#1C1463) or Slate (#83858A) for text
- **Accent:** Sunset (#FF331F) for call-to-action elements
